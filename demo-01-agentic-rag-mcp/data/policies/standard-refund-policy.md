# Standard Refund Policy

This policy governs standard refunds for completed purchases. It applies to most consumer orders unless a more specific policy (such as subscriptions, partial orders, or fraud disputes) takes precedence.

## Refund Window

Customers may request a refund within **30 days** of the order date. The 30-day window begins on the date the payment was successfully captured, not the date the item shipped. Requests submitted after this window are handled under the expired refund window policy.

## Eligibility Criteria

An order is eligible for a standard refund when all of the following are true:

- The payment was successfully captured and not already reversed.
- The order is within the 30-day window.
- No prior refund has been issued against the order.
- The order is not in a status that blocks refunds (see order status definitions).

## Standard Process

1. Verify the customer's identity and locate the order.
2. Confirm eligibility against the criteria above.
3. Submit the refund to the original payment method.
4. Record the refund reference number on the order.
5. Notify the customer of the approved amount and expected timeline.

## What the Customer Receives

A standard refund returns the full amount paid for the eligible order, including any tax collected, to the original payment method. Shipping fees are refunded only when the entire order is returned or cancelled before fulfillment.

If only part of an order qualifies, see the partial refund policy. If the original payment method is no longer valid, escalate so an alternative disbursement can be arranged. Customers should be told that processing times vary by payment method.
